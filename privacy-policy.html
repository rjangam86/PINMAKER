Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 11, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how PINMAKER handles user data in the process of automating Pinterest publishing.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only non-personal data such as product URLs, image sources, and affiliate links necessary to generate and post Pins. No personal data is stored or processed.

2. Use of Information

The information collected is used exclusively to automate the generation of Pinterest Pins using affiliate content. We do not sell, share, or store user data beyond temporary automation sessions.

3. Data Storage

Data is processed in-memory during execution. No long-term storage or cookies are used.

4. Third-Party Links

Pins generated by PINMAKER may include affiliate links that redirect to third-party retailers. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites.

For any privacy-related inquiries, contact us at: contact@pinmaker.app
